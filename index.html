Meet Your Chiropractor

Dr. Liz Borham

Dr. Liz sees her role as a chiropractor as being a partner with people who want to achieve health and wellness goals. She loves chiropractic for its focus on health from the 'inside out' and she loves ABC for its powerful, consistent, measurable results.
